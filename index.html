About

Phumela Group is a 100% female, black-owned and managed professional services and accounting firm. Phumela derives its meaning from “Umphumela Omuhle,” an IsiZulu phrase meaning excellent result. This definition embodies one of the core values of the Phumela Group as a whole – to push boundaries and provide unique and exceptional services to clients. Due to the size of Phumela, every client receives a personalized professional experience.

  • Values

    Phumela is committed to the highest standard of integrity, equality, excellence and professionalism. A rich heritage, combined with robust leadership guides the firm in its vision, mission, and values.

  • Mission

    Our mission is to support, develop and empower South African organizations through compliant, high quality, and holistic professional services.

  • Vision

    >Our vision is to become a value-adding, leading South African service provider of Accounting, Taxation, and Business Advisory services.
